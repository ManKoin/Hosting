Meet Pixel, Phone by Google.

It has the highest rated smartphone camera. Ever. A battery that lasts all day. Unlimited storage for all your photos and videos. And it’s the first phone with the Google Assistant built in.

- The first phone with the Google Assistant built in.
Know when local places close, if it's still going to rain on Saturday, and if your favorite team won last night.

- The highest rated smartphone camera. Ever.
With a best-ever 89 DxOMark Mobile score, Pixel's camera lets you take brilliant photos in low light, bright light or any light.

PHONE SPECS

- Unlimited storage for all your photos and videos.
Say goodbye to those “storage is full” notifications.

- Your closest friends, closer.
With Google Duo you can video call with friends and family on Android & iOS®.

- An experience made by Google.
The Pixel combines hardware and software made by Google, including all your favorite Google apps, to bring you a fast, seamless, and easy-to-use experience.

- Make it yours.

Choose from a customized Live Case with a matching live wallpaper or one designed with Google Earth and Google Trends. Or grab the Pixel Case by Google, built for protection and featuring a range of bright, bold colors.

- Built for VR.
Daydream View is a VR headset and controller by Google that lets you explore new worlds, kick back in your personal VR cinema, and play games that put you at the center of action.

Pre-order Pixel and enjoy the new Daydream View VR headset on us.

- Two sizes. Three colors.

5” or 5.5”. Quite Black, Very Silver, or Really Blue.

- The best of Google on the next-gen network by Verizon.
The largest and most reliable network in the US. Play games, stream shows, download music — faster and easier.
